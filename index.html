Automations

Workflow Automations Built

Three production automation systems handling inbound call routing, SMS intent detection, and lead and vendor notification pipelines at scale.

Multi-Channel Inbound Call Routing System
TwilioVoice RoutingCRM IntegrationAfter-Hours Logic
Overview
A full inbound call handling architecture built across four separate workflows covering Customer Ops Primary, Dial Status, Voicemail Transcription, and PCR Marketing for customer and vendor acquisition. Each flow handles routing logic, call logging, voicemail transcription via SMS, and fallback escalation after business hours.
What This System Does
  • Routes inbound calls through business hours logic, primary agent, then after-hours fallback with SMS alert.
  • Logs every call with disposition updates via POST webhooks to a connected CRM.
  • Transcribes voicemails and delivers them to the primary contact via SMS automatically.
  • Handles vendor acquisition calls with a multi-step IVR collecting company, cities, services, and size before confirming and routing.
  • Separate marketing acquisition flow with lead qualification greeting and redirect to AI gather link.
Lead and Vendor Notification Pipeline
Lead RoutingVendor OnboardingEmail AutomationApproval Flows
Overview
A multi-branch notification and approval workflow covering new lead intake, vendor onboarding, vendor approvals, lead acceptance and rejection notifications, lead planned alerts, vendor withdrawal handling, and Mailchimp spot audits. Each branch triggers conditional logic based on entity state and routes to the correct notification handler.
What This System Does
  • New lead notifications fire immediately on capture and route to the assigned team member.
  • Vendor approval flow checks entity state, notifies primary on approval, and triggers onboarding sequence.
  • Lead accepted and rejected notifications keep all parties informed without manual follow-up.
  • Vendor withdrawal handling updates CRM records and alerts the primary contact via SMS.
  • Mailchimp spot audit branch runs periodic list health checks and notifies on anomalies.
Automated SMS Routing and Intent Detection System
SMS AutomationIntent DetectionKeyword BranchingOpt-in / Opt-out
Overview
An automated SMS routing system deployed across 4 phone numbers that detects inbound message intent using keyword branching and responds dynamically while logging all interactions. Handles STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT for opt-out compliance, HELP for support routing, and JOIN for opt-in campaigns.
What This System Does
  • Detects SMS intent on arrival using keyword branching across STOP, HELP, JOIN, and all other message types.
  • STOP and opt-out keywords trigger compliance responses and Twilio configuration updates automatically.
  • HELP routes to support info reply per active campaign without manual agent involvement.
  • JOIN processes opt-in, logs the event, and enrolls the contact in the relevant campaign.
  • All other messages perform entity lookup, assess if substantive, and alert primary or respond quietly based on the result.
